Aura Browser Extension Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

This page describes how the Aura browser extension ("the Extension") collects, uses, and stores your data. By installing or using the Extension, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data we collect

1.1 Account information (optional)

When you sign up for an Aura account or log in, we collect your name, email address, and password. Passwords are used only for authentication and are stored on the server as secure hashes, not in plain text.

1.2 Tab, collection, and workspace data

To provide tab and workspace management and sync, the Extension stores metadata about your browsing structure both locally in your browser and, if you choose to sign in, on our servers. This includes workspace and collection names, ordering, and metadata about saved tabs such as page titles, URLs, favicons, pinned status, and timestamps.

1.3 Device and network information

When you use our backend for authentication or sync, our infrastructure provider (Cloudflare) may process technical data such as IP address, approximate location (for example, country or region), and basic browser or device information to provide security and reliability for the service.

2. How we use your data

We use the data described above solely to provide and improve the Aura service, including:

  • Providing and maintaining core tab, collection, and workspace features
  • Synchronizing your data across devices when you sign in
  • Performing authentication and security checks
  • Debugging issues and improving stability and user experience

We do not sell your personal data to third parties and we do not use your data for targeted advertising.

3. Data storage and security

Account and sync data are stored in databases hosted on Cloudflare with appropriate technical and organizational security measures. Passwords are never stored in plain text and are kept only as secure hashes. We retain your data for as long as needed to operate the service and comply with legal obligations. Where applicable, you may request deletion of your account and associated data.

4. Your choices and control

  • You can use the Extension without signing in, in which case data stays on your local device.
  • You can sign out from within the Extension and optionally clear local data.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

5. Third-party services

The Extension relies on Cloudflare to host the backend API, database, and authentication layer. These providers may process limited technical data such as IP addresses under their own privacy policies in order to deliver security, performance, and reliability.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, provide additional notice within the Extension or on our website.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at: phyzess@gmail.com.

Aura 浏览器扩展隐私政策

最近更新日期：2025-01-01

本页面说明 Aura 浏览器扩展（下称「本扩展」）如何收集、使用和存储您的数据。安装或使用本扩展，即表示您同意本隐私政策中的做法。

一、我们收集哪些数据

1. 账号信息（可选）

当您注册或登录 Aura 账号时，我们会收集您的姓名、电子邮箱地址以及密码。密码仅用于身份验证，服务器端会以安全方式存储其散列值，而不会以明文形式保存。

2. 标签、集合和工作区数据

为提供标签和工作区管理及同步功能，本扩展会在浏览器本地以及（在您选择登录的情况下）我们的服务器中存储与您的浏览结构相关的元数据，包括工作区和集合的名称、排序，以及已保存标签的标题、URL、图标、是否固定以及时间戳等信息。

3. 设备与网络相关信息

当您使用我们的后端进行登录或同步时，基础设施提供商（Cloudflare）可能会处理例如 IP 地址、大致地理位置（如国家或地区）以及浏览器或设备的基础信息，以保障服务的安全性与可靠性。

二、我们如何使用这些数据

我们仅将上述数据用于提供和改进 Aura 服务，包括：

  • 提供并维护标签、集合和工作区等核心功能
  • 在您登录的情况下，在多设备之间同步数据
  • 执行必要的身份验证和安全审计
  • 排查问题并改进稳定性和用户体验

我们不会将您的个人数据出售给任何第三方，也不会将其用于定向广告投放。

三、数据存储与安全

账号和同步数据存储在由 Cloudflare 托管的数据库中，并采取适当的技术和组织措施进行保护。密码不会以明文形式存储，而是以安全的散列值形式保存。我们会在提供服务及履行法律义务所需的期间内保留您的数据，在适用的情况下，您可以请求删除账号及相关数据。

四、您的选择与控制

  • 您可以在不登录的情况下使用本扩展，此时数据仅保存在本地设备中。
  • 您可以在扩展内随时退出登录，并选择清除本地数据。
  • 如需访问、更正或删除您的个人数据，您可以通过下方联系方式与我们取得联系。

五、第三方服务

本扩展依赖 Cloudflare 提供后端 API、数据库和身份验证服务。这些服务提供商可能会根据各自的隐私政策处理例如 IP 地址等有限的技术数据，以保证安全性、性能和可靠性。

六、本政策的更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。当政策发生变更时，我们会更新本页面顶部的「最近更新日期」，并在适当情况下在扩展或网站中提供额外提示。

七、联系我们

如对本隐私政策或您的数据有任何疑问，请联系：phyzess@gmail.com。
